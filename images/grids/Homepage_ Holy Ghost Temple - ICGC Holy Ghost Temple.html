Our Senior Pastor

Prophet Christopher Yaw Annor is the Senior Pastor of the Holy Ghost Temple of the International Central Gospel Church. His Prophetic ministry has changed many lives in the nation, and the world at large.

Solution Centre

The Prophetic Solution Centre is a time of deep prayer and intense worship that creates an atmosphere of solutions and miracles. Make a date with Prophet Christopher Yaw Annor every Thursday at 10:30am.

Children's Ministry

Our Children Ministry is grouped into various classes to cater for children of all ages. We have over 100 Facilitators who teach and care for your child as you worship in the Adult Service. Every child fits right in.

Youth Ministry

We have a dynamic Youth Ministry tailored to meet the needs of every youth. Our Youth Service is divided into two sections; the Junior Youth, and the Senior Youth to ensure that each age group is properly mentored and catered for.
